Preparation Notes and Description

Over time my cooking has evolved as I found my own style. I naturally gravitated to charcuterie in the form of bacon and fresh sausages first. Then I discovered wild berries, which ignited my interest in all types of fruits in jars. You've all recieved random batches of both these types of things as I've made something worth sharing. So now, rather than work on food that fits rigedly to a theme. I cook what I like. Hawaii. The Southwest. Argentina. Those places all shaped who I am and hsow through in my food.
